About

A storyteller who builds worlds from imagination and innovation.

Sein Lin — known professionally as Sammy Linn — is a passionate filmmaker and creative director based in Myanmar. As Co-Founder and Creative Director of Aliens Productions since 2018, he has led the company from a three-person startup to a full creative studio, working with global brands like Lenovo, Telenor, and Unilever.

His versatility spans directing, writing, VFX supervision, editing, and production management — delivering projects that push the boundaries of visual storytelling in the Myanmar film and television industry.
